About SALONFIX

Meet Jawhar Raj Rajkumar

The driving force behind SALONFIX. Passionate about helping salon owners achieve operational excellence and financial clarity.

Our Story

After years of witnessing salon owners struggle with revenue leaks, mismanaged operations, and lack of clarity in their finances, Jawhar Raj Rajkumar created SALONFIX with one mission: to run your salon like it’s our own.

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted in-house partner for salons, SALONFIX is redefining how salon businesses are managed – blending daily operational oversight with growth strategy.

Our Mission

We believe every salon owner deserves to focus on creativity and clients, while a reliable partner handles the numbers, systems, and growth strategy. SALONFIX is that partner.

Audit & Diagnose

Every profit leak gets uncovered.

  • Review sales, staff, stock, and client return rates.
  • Find leaks: petty cash misuse, vendor overpricing, wastage.
  • Spot weak systems: late staff, no rebooking, hygiene slips.
  • Deliver a clear baseline profit report.

Plug the Leaks

Every leak gets a lock.

  • Petty cash → every spend logged with proof.
  • Vendors → bills checked, savings unlocked with negotiation.
  • Stock → usage matched with billing, wastage closed.
  • Expenses → tracked daily to stop hidden drains.

Systemize Daily Operations

Chaos turns into daily control.

  • SOP routines: opening, mid-shift, evening, closing.
  • Staff proof photos: salon opened, cleaning done, POS reports.
  • Daily report → one WhatsApp snapshot.
  • Owner Action Box → repairs, purchases, clear next steps.

Train & Align Team

Your team becomes your system.

  • Rebooking scripts + reminders (chair, 24h, 72h).
  • Retail push: 2-item rule + mirror talk.
  • Hygiene standards with proof of compliance.
  • Incentives linked to rebooking %, retail %, and daily targets.

Monitor & Scale Profit

Profit clarity, branch by branch.

  • Daily: WhatsApp report → sales, profit, rebooking %, retail %, pending issues.
  • Weekly: Team performance review + Owner Action Box.
  • Monthly: Income vs expenses deep-dive + profit projection.
  • Multi-branch ready: one system across outlets with no loss of control.

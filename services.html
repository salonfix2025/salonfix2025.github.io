1

Audit & Diagnose

  • Review sales, staff, stock, and client return rates.
  • Identify leaks: petty cash misuse, vendor overpricing, stock wastage.
  • Spot weak systems: late staff, no rebooking, hygiene slips.
  • Owner receives a clear baseline report.
2

Plug the Leaks

  • Petty cash → every spend logged with receipt/photo proof.
  • Vendors → bills cross-checked, savings unlocked with negotiation.
  • Stock → usage matched with billing, wastage stopped.
  • Expenses → tracked daily to prevent hidden drains.
3

Systemize Daily Operations

  • SOP routines: opening, mid-shift, evening, closing.
  • Staff post proof photos: salon opened, cleaning done, POS reports, petty cash counts.
  • Daily report compiled into a one-page WhatsApp update.
  • Owner Action Box → repairs, purchases, and clear next steps.
4

Train & Align Team

  • Rebooking system: scripts + reminders (chair, 24h, 72h).
  • Retail push: 2-item rule + mirror talk method.
  • Hygiene standards: staff grooming + workstation readiness with proof.
  • Incentives tied to rebooking %, retail %, and daily targets.
5

Monitor & Scale Profit

  • Daily: WhatsApp report → sales, profit, rebooking %, retail %, pending issues.
  • Weekly: team performance review + owner action box.
  • Monthly: income vs expenses deep-dive + profit projection.
  • Multi-branch ready: same system scales across outlets with no loss of control.

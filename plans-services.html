WHICH LEVEL OF TAKEOVER IS RIGHT FOR YOUR SALON?

Not every salon has the same challenges. Some need a reset, some need daily control, and some — with multiple branches — need one unified system running across every location.

SALONFIX has four flexible packages — designed to fit any stage of your salon journey.

Our Packages

START STRONG – System Setup & Reset

  • Best for new or relaunching salons.
  • Full SOP setup, initial stock audit, and front desk coaching.
  • One-time fee: ₹25K–₹50K (includes 1–2 weeks setup).
  • Purpose: Get your salon’s backend organized from Day 1.

STAY SHARP – Ongoing Operations Control

  • Best for busy owners who need daily oversight.
  • Weekly or bi-weekly visits, monthly dashboards, inventory oversight.
  • Price: ₹30K–₹75K/month.
  • Purpose: Keep your salon on track, without you micromanaging.

STYLE EMPIRE – Multi-Branch Ops

  • Best for franchise groups or multi-location owners.
  • Cross-branch SOP design, centralized reporting, and multi-location vendor strategy.
  • Price: Starting ₹75K/month (custom, based on scale).
  • Purpose: One system for every outlet — your entire network runs the same way.

LIGHT TOUCH – Remote Strategic Support

  • Best for small, owner-managed salons.
  • WhatsApp data reviews, guidance-only format, and remote fixes.
  • Price: ₹15K/month.
  • Purpose: Light advisory support — systems without daily visits.

ONE FORMAT. ONE SYSTEM. EVERY BRANCH.

Running multiple salons shouldn’t mean managing multiple headaches.

SALONFIX creates a single, unified system for all your outlets — so whether you have 2 branches or 20, the same standards, data, and controls are at your fingertips every day.

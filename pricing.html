PLANS & SERVICES

Not every salon has the same challenges. Some need a reset, some need daily control, and some — with multiple branches — need one unified system running across every location.

1. START STRONG – System Setup & Reset

  • Best for new or relaunching salons.
  • Full SOP setup, initial stock audit, and front desk coaching.
  • One-time fee: ₹25K–₹50K (includes 1–2 weeks setup).

2. STAY SHARP – Ongoing Operations Control

  • Best for busy owners who need daily oversight.
  • Weekly or bi-weekly visits, monthly dashboards, inventory oversight.
  • Price: ₹30K–₹75K/month.

3. STYLE EMPIRE – Multi-Branch Ops

  • Best for franchise groups or multi-location owners.
  • Cross-branch SOP design, centralized reporting, and multi-location vendor strategy.
  • Price: Starting ₹75K/month.

4. LIGHT TOUCH – Remote Strategic Support

  • Best for small, owner-managed salons.
  • WhatsApp data reviews, guidance-only format, and remote fixes.
  • Price: ₹15K/month.
